Beginning of it all

Welcome to the start of it all. What started as a way for me to learn about web dev using HTML and CSS turned into a full on blog. Oops.

As of writing this right now, i still don't quite know if it will be published anywhere on the interwebs. After all, i don't exactly have anything important to say, and i sure as hell cannot think of anyone that actually wants to read this. But as far as pandemic activities go, this one is not so bad I guess.

What will this blog be about? Who knows. Themes are hard. There might be a spot of mental health discussion here, a smidge of computer programming stuff there, a smattering of politics hidden in some corner. I definitely do not expect any type of unity or organisation, but I may one day implement some sort of tagging system. Again, i am not expecting anyone to actually be reading this, so if you are, congrats for betraying my extremely low expectations.

Signing off for now.

-Kip, 22 May 2020
