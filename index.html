A HackMIT 2020 project! Our team created this prototype in just a couple of hours. Based on our own experiences with remote learning, we thought it would be great for folks to find friends to watch lecture videos with without actually having to schedule in advance.

Dynamic Image Croppping - Seam Carving

Dynamic image cropping underlies modern commercial content-aware scaling algorithms. Seam carving removes the "least energy seam," which is a path of continuous pixels where the difference in luminosity between pixels in the path and neighbour pixels are minimised.
